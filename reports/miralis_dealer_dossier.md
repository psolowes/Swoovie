## Price / Cost / Durability / Value Assessment
### Price Driver Heuristics
- Material ladder typically moves Laminate < Similacquer < Lacquer < Advanced Engineering/FENIX or premium wood selections.
- Door profile complexity (45° assemblies, finger grip machining, wood species) and finish process elevate labor cost.
- Accessory density (lighting, curio integrations, premium drawer runners) increases system cost.
- Custom colors and grain-alignment requests are likely quote multipliers.

### Durability Signals
- Humidity resistance is explicitly marketed for Similacquer.
- European laminate is described as very resistant with matching interior finish program.
- Maintenance guide indicates regular hinge tuning as part of expected normal use.
- Chemical storage practices can influence corrosion and long-term hardware life.
